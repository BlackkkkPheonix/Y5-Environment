📖 HOW TO PLAY

Become the Ultimate Eco-Champion

🌱
Starting Cash: Every player starts with 300🌱. Pass the START space to collect 200🌱.
🏘️
Eco-Properties (Green): Buy and upgrade them to collect rent/charges. They earn a bonus of +30🌱 per level at the end of the game!
🏭
Polluting Properties (Red): Buy to earn income each time you land. You can Decommission at any time to gain Eco Points (small cleanup fee applies). At end game they incur a government penalty.
☢️
Nuclear Plants (Yellow): High-risk investment. Gives a massive +800🌱 end-game bonus, but has a 5% chance of meltdown each turn, costing you up to 600🌱 in repairs.
💡
Quiz & Event Spaces: Land on Quiz tiles to answer environmental questions and earn +200🌱! Event tiles trigger random global effects.
🚨
Pollution Detention (Jail): Catching a pollution penalty sends you to Detention. Roll a 5 or 6 to escape on parole, or pay 100🌱 to bail out immediately.
🏆
Winning the Game: The player with the highest final wealth (including end-game property bonuses/fines) when time runs out is crowned the Eco-Champion! Negative cash (debt) is allowed.
🚨

Pollution Detention!

You were caught violating environmental guidelines! You can pay a 100🌱 fine to get out immediately, or risk going to detention and rolling for parole on your next turns.

🌱 ABOUT ECOPOLIS

Behind the Green Revolution

💡
Why I Made This Game:

I created EcoPolis to show that environmental issues aren't just something adults talk about — they affect everyone, including us. I wanted a game that puts real decisions in your hands: do you invest in clean energy, or take the money from polluting industries? The goal was to make those trade-offs feel real, not just like a classroom exercise.

🌍
Why Environmental Awareness Matters:

Carbon emissions, deforestation, and plastic pollution are measurable problems with real consequences — rising temperatures, loss of biodiversity, and more extreme weather events. Scientists agree that action needs to happen now. Understanding these issues is the first step toward making smarter choices, both in games and in real life.

🛠️
How I Built It:

EcoPolis was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the core languages of the web. The game board, player movement, property system, and trivia engine were all coded from scratch. Sound effects are generated in real time using the Web Audio API, meaning no audio files are required. The project was developed with the assistance of Google Antigravity, an AI coding tool that helped me plan, write, and debug the code throughout the process.

🏆
Why You Should Play:

EcoPolis is a strategy game where every decision has consequences. You can build a sustainable economy and earn a large government bonus at the end, or chase short-term profits through polluting industries and face carbon tax penalties. With environmental trivia, surprise event cards, nuclear plant risks, and up to 6 players — every game plays out differently. The player who balances profit and sustainability best wins.
